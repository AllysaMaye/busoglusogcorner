About Busog Lusog Corner

Located inside Sta. Teresa College near the canteen, Busog Lusog Corner serves healthy and affordable snacks made with local ingredients like malunggay, carrots, and calamansi.

Our mission is to promote well-balanced eating habits while keeping every bite busog sa sarap at lusog sa bawat kagat.
